English Learning Advices

Read Every Day

Reading English books, articles, or news daily will help you expand your vocabulary and understand sentence structures.

Watch English Movies

Watching films or series in English with subtitles improves listening skills and pronunciation in a fun way.

Practice Speaking

Speak English every day, even with yourself, to gain fluency and confidence in forming sentences.

Use Language Apps

Apps like Duolingo or Babbel are excellent tools to practice vocabulary, grammar, and daily exercises.

Write a Journal

Write short paragraphs or daily journals in English to improve your writing and expression skills.

Listen to Podcasts

English podcasts and audiobooks help you develop listening comprehension and familiarize yourself with natural speech.
